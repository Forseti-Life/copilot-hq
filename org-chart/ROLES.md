# Roles

This org uses a small set of roles to enforce checks-and-balances.

## CEO (Copilot Orchestration)
- Coordinates work across websites and teams; enforces ownership boundaries.
- Docs: `org-chart/roles/ceo.md`, `org-chart/roles/ceo.instructions.md`

## Product Manager (PM)
- Owns Drupal **module-level** scope, acceptance criteria, and ship/no-ship recommendation.
- Docs: `org-chart/roles/product-manager.md`, `org-chart/roles/product-manager.instructions.md`

## Business Analyst (BA)
- Bridges the gap between technical execution and business goals by translating complex client requirements into structured technical specifications.
- Docs: `org-chart/roles/business-analyst.md`, `org-chart/roles/business-analyst.instructions.md`

## Software Developer (Dev)
- Implements the smallest viable change to satisfy acceptance criteria; documents tests/verification.
- Docs: `org-chart/roles/software-developer.md`, `org-chart/roles/software-developer.instructions.md`

## Tester (QA)
- Independently verifies acceptance criteria and failure modes; issues APPROVE/BLOCK.
- Docs: `org-chart/roles/tester.md`, `org-chart/roles/tester.instructions.md`

## Security Analyst
- Proactively identifies security/privacy risks across code, configs, and automation; recommends mitigations and verification steps.
- Docs: `org-chart/roles/security-analyst.md`, `org-chart/roles/security-analyst.instructions.md`

## Accountant
- Owns financial operations visibility for product income and spend, reconciles billing and cash records, tracks financial commitments, and recommends control or optimization actions to leadership.
- Docs: `org-chart/roles/accountant.md`, `org-chart/roles/accountant.instructions.md`

## Source of Truth
- Shipping gates: `runbooks/shipping-gates.md`
- Cross-module passthrough: `runbooks/passthrough-request.md`
- Conflict resolution: `runbooks/conflict-resolution.md`

- Decision/RACI matrix: `org-chart/DECISION_OWNERSHIP_MATRIX.md`
