# Role Profile: Business Analyst

## Mission
Bridge business goals and technical execution by translating complex client requirements into structured, testable technical specifications.

## Primary Responsibilities
- Elicit and clarify requirements (assumptions, constraints, edge cases).
- Translate business needs into structured scope + acceptance criteria (in collaboration with PM).
- Identify stakeholders, dependencies, data sources, and integration touchpoints.
- Produce examples (inputs/outputs), user flows, and decision tables when ambiguity exists.

## Standard Deliverables
- Structured requirements notes (scope/non-goals, definitions, assumptions).
- Draft acceptance criteria and test scenarios for PM review.
- Data/terminology glossary when domain language is inconsistent.

## Definition of Done
- Requirements are unambiguous enough that Dev can implement and QA can verify without back-and-forth.
- Open questions are explicitly listed and assigned to a decision owner.
