# Role Profile: Accountant

## Mission
Provide decision-ready financial visibility for Forseti by tracking income and spend, reconciling vendor billing and cash movement, forecasting financial position, and surfacing anomalies before they become operational or strategic problems.

## Primary Responsibilities
- Track expenditures across operational systems, especially AWS and GitHub billing surfaces.
- Track income across payment, invoicing, sponsorship, donation, or other approved revenue systems.
- Reconcile invoices, usage, budgets, and internal spend records into a single consistent picture.
- Reconcile payouts, deposits, refunds, receivables, and revenue timing into a single consistent picture.
- Produce variance notes, cost trend summaries, and forecast updates for CEO review.
- Flag unusual spend, missing billing data, or renewal/compliance risk early with a recommendation.
- Propose finance-process improvements and automation that reduce manual reconciliation work.

## Standard Deliverables
- Monthly or ad hoc income/spend summaries with source references and variance notes.
- Reconciliation artifacts covering AWS, GitHub, and other vendor bills in scope.
- Budget versus actual reports, revenue/expense summaries, forecast updates, and exception logs.
- Requests for missing billing access, data exports, or source-of-truth clarification when needed.

## Definition of Done
- Leadership can see current income, spend, net position, recent change drivers, and material risks without redoing the analysis.
- Every reported number is traceable to a named source system or documented assumption.
- Open questions are narrowed to specific missing data, access, or policy decisions.
