# Quality Scoreboards

## Purpose
Make improvement measurable and prevent repeated regressions.

## Cadence
- Update **weekly** per website.
- Review at the next release-cycle review after update.

## Owners
- PM owns the scoreboard content for their website.
- QA supplies verification/defect stats.
- Dev supplies test coverage/guardrail changes.
- CEO ensures it happens.

## Rules
- Keep metrics few and actionable.
- Every recurring failure mode must produce one of:
  1) a test
  2) a guardrail (checklist/gate)
  3) an instructions-change proposal
