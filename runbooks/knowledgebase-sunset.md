# Knowledgebase Sunset (Prune + Update)

## Purpose
Prevent the knowledgebase from becoming a graveyard of outdated rules.

## Cadence
- **Weekly (30 min):** prune duplicates, add tags/links, ensure each lesson has prevention steps.
- **Monthly (60 min):** consolidate similar lessons into one canonical playbook.
- **Quarterly (90 min):** remove obsolete guidance; update templates/runbooks based on what actually worked.

## Required outputs
- Mark stale lessons with a short note at top: "Status: stale" and why.
- Convert frequently referenced lessons into a playbook section within the lesson.
- Create/refresh proposals when instruction gaps persist.
