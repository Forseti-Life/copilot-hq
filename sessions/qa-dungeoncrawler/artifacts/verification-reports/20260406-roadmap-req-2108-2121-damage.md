## Defects

### DEF-2114: min-1 damage — logic bug (never fires on full resistance)
- **File**: `HPManager.php`
- **Line**: ~2200 (`$original_damage = $damage` after resistance loop)
- **Root cause**: `$original_damage` is captured after resistance is applied. When `resistance >= incoming_damage`, `$damage` becomes 0, `$original_damage` becomes 0, and the guard `if ($original_damage > 0 && $damage < 1)` evaluates FALSE. Min-1 never applies.
- **Fix**: Capture `$incoming_damage` before the resistance loop. Change guard to `if ($incoming_damage > 0 && $damage < 1)`.
- **Severity**: Medium — affects any combat scenario where resistance equals or exceeds incoming damage.

### GAP-2111/2112: thrown/propulsive Str modifier not applied in CombatEngine
- **File**: `CombatEngine.php` (resolveAttack, line ~507)
- **Root cause**: CE uses `$weapon['ability_modifier']` directly. `ItemCombatDataService` provides `damage_str_mode` but CE never reads it. The caller or CE must translate `damage_str_mode` + character Str → actual `ability_modifier` for damage.
- **Severity**: Medium — thrown weapons and propulsive ranged weapons do not apply Str modifier to damage.

### GAP-2116: CritSpecializationService not called on critical hits
- **File**: `CombatEngine.php` (resolveAttack, critical_success branch)
- **Root cause**: `CritSpecializationService` is implemented as a standalone service but never injected or called from `CombatEngine::resolveAttack()`.
- **Severity**: Low — crit specialization effects (weapon group bleed, prone, etc.) silently absent on crits.

### GAP-2118: critical_hits immunity not applied in CombatEngine
- **File**: `CombatEngine.php` (resolveAttack, degree calculation)
- **Root cause**: CE never calls `RulesEngine::checkImmunities('critical_hits', ...)` before doubling damage. Creatures with critical-hit immunity still take doubled damage.
- **Severity**: Medium — crit-immune creatures take double damage incorrectly.

### GAP-2119: precision immunity not stripped in CombatEngine
- **File**: `CombatEngine.php` (resolveAttack, damage application)
- **Root cause**: CE never calls `RulesEngine::checkImmunities('precision', ...)`. Precision damage is not stripped for immune targets.
- **Severity**: Low-medium — precision-immune creatures (constructs, elementals, oozes) take full precision damage incorrectly.
