## Description
Implement the rune system: fundamental runes (potency, striking, resilient), property runes, transferring runes, etching runes to items. Also: special materials (adamantine, cold iron, silver, mithral, etc.) with material grades (low/standard/high). Covers core/ch11 rune and materials sections.
